# Wanted Level Clearing

Active methods for players to reduce their Wanted level faster than passive time-based decay.

## Requirements

- [ ] Wanted level active clearing
  - [ ] Bribe — pay a guard or fixer NPC to reduce Wanted level by 1; cost scales with current level
  - [ ] Quest-based clearing — complete a quest for a faction or NPC that results in Wanted level reduction
  - [ ] Surrender — turn yourself in to a guard; serves time (in-game hours advance) to clear one level
