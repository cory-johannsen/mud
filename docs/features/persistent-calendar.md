# Persistent Calendar

An in-game calendar that advances with time, driving time-of-day and seasonal events.

## Requirements

- [] Persistent calendar
  - Day & Month
  - Displayed on screen
    - Move time from prompt to room section, after the room name (on the same line as the room name)
    - Add Month and day to time display in the format "January 1st", preceding the time of day 
  - Drives seasons and weather
    - 4 seasons follow standard calendar rules
    - Weather effects 
      - determined by season
      - scale with room danger level [requires Room Danger Levels]
