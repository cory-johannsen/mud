# Brothel Expansion

Adds a `brothel_keeper` NPC type and a dedicated brothel room to every zone. Brothels offer cheaper rest than motels with risk/reward trade-offs: 15% disease chance (random from a pool of 10 brothel-specific diseases), 20% robbery chance (5% crypto + items), and a 1-day +1 Flair bonus on success. Each brothel also houses a black market merchant and relocates the zone's Fixer.
