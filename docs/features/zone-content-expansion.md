# Zone Content Expansion

Expands all existing zones with additional rooms, per-room danger level variation, diversified combat NPC types and distributions, and improved lore density. Dependent on `non-combat-npcs-all-zones` (safe room NPCs must be in place first).

## Requirements

- [ ] Zone content expansion
  - Per-zone room expansion (additional rooms, better map coverage)
  - Per-room danger level variation (rooms within a zone escalate toward the zone core)
  - Diversified combat NPC types per zone (unique enemy flavors per zone theme)
  - Improved NPC distribution (density and placement relative to danger level)
  - One lore-appropriate `chip_doc` NPC placed in a Safe room per zone (required for curse-removal)
